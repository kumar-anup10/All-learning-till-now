Faith, dutifulness, compassion, good thoughts, character, non-attachment, helping nature, fearlessness, love, wisdom, spirituality, responsibility, truth, strength within, the company of the good etc. are some traits which are recommended by Vivekananda for personality development.

Vivekananda believed that every soul is destined for perfection and will ultimately attain a state of perfection. A human being has five layers or sheaths - physical, vital, mental, intellectual, and blissful. Today's education can develop the first four sheaths but not the fifth, which requires spiritual knowledge.
